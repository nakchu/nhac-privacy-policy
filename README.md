# NHAC Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Introduction

NHAC ("the App") is a mobile application designed to provide healthcare professionals with access to schedules, reference materials, calculators, contact information, and other clinical resources.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

The App may allow users to authenticate using Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.

When you sign in, Google and Firebase may provide the App with certain account information, including:

* Name
* Email address
* Unique account identifier

This information is used solely for authentication and access control purposes.

---

## Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store certain information locally on your device, including:

* Login preferences
* Biometric authentication settings
* App preferences
* Cached reference materials
* Cached schedule information

This information remains on your device and is not sold or shared by the developer.

---

## Biometric Authentication

The App may support biometric authentication (such as Face ID or fingerprint authentication) to simplify future sign-ins.

Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system and is never accessed, collected, stored, or transmitted by the App or its developer.

---

## Schedule and Reference Content

The App may display:

* Work schedules
* Contact information
* Clinical references
* Forms
* Educational resources
* Links to third-party websites and services

The developer does not control the privacy practices of third-party websites and encourages users to review their respective privacy policies.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

### Google Firebase

Firebase may be used for:

* User authentication
* App functionality
* Security services

Firebase services are provided by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

### Google Sign-In

Google Sign-In is used to authenticate users and provide secure access to the App.

### Google Play Services

Google Play Services may collect technical information necessary to provide app functionality, security, crash reporting, and platform services.

---

## Data Sharing

The developer does not:

* Sell personal information
* Rent personal information
* Share personal information with advertisers
* Use personal information for marketing purposes

Information is only shared when necessary to provide authentication or other functionality through integrated third-party services.

---

## Data Retention

Authentication information is retained only as necessary to maintain access to the App and provide requested services.

Locally stored information remains on your device until it is removed by you, deleted through the App, or removed when the App is uninstalled.

---

## Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect information processed through the App.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is intended for healthcare professionals and adult users.

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## International Users

Information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence through the use of third-party services such as Google and Firebase.

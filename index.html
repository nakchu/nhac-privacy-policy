NHAC Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

NHAC ("the App") is a mobile application designed to provide healthcare professionals with access to schedules, reference materials, calculators, contact information, and other clinical resources.

Information We Collect

The App may use Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication to authenticate users. Information provided by Google may include your name, email address, and account identifier.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store login preferences, biometric settings, cached schedules, and reference materials locally on your device.

Biometric Authentication

Biometric authentication such as Face ID or fingerprint authentication is processed entirely by your device and is never collected or stored by the App.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Play Services

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for healthcare professionals and is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.
